]]> 2391 Giulio Rampa thesis prize https://davidegerosa.com/2018/09/10/giulio-rampa-thesis-prize/?utm_source=rss&utm_medium=rss&utm_campaign=giulio-rampa-thesis-prize Mon, 10 Sep 2018 14:41:19 +0000 http://davidegerosa.com/?p=2201 Continue reading →]]> I was recently awarded the 2018 Giulio Rampa Thesis Prize for Outstanding Research in General Relativity. Dr. Gerosa’s Ph.D. Thesis on “Source modelling at the dawn of gravitational-wave astronomy” shows an impressive ability to master a rather broad range of topics in relativistic astrophysics and gravitational wave physics. The large impact that the work of Dr. Gerosa has already had can only continue to grow.
